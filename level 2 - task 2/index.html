Biography


Mahendra Singh Dhoni is a former Indian cricketer and one of the most successful cricket captains in the history of Indian cricket. He was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, India. Dhoni is primarily known for his exceptional skills as a wicket-keeper batsman and is considered one of the best finishers in limited-overs cricket.

Dhoni was widely admired for his calm and composed demeanor on the field, which earned him the nickname "Captain Cool." His sharp cricketing brain and innovative captaincy tactics made him a highly respected figure in the cricketing world.

Outside of cricket, Dhoni is also known for his various philanthropic endeavors, business ventures, and love for sports like football and motorsports and also MS Dhoni held the honorary rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the Indian Territorial Army.
